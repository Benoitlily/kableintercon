AboutUs

KABLE INTERCON TECH prides itself in having a highly qualified team with wealthy of experience within the security industry. The company was established with the objective of bridging the gap between customer expectations and actual value delivered. The ultimate goal is to give our customers peace of mind through innovative custom-made and solution driven electronic security solutions by utilising and applying our unmatched product knowledge, design skills and integration techniques.
