ACCESS CONTROL

system integration with employee and visitor ​management

As KABLE INTERCON TECH, we help organisations to secure their businesses with biometrics by providing modern access control systems that are integrated with artificial intelligence in order to enhance the safety of our customers. Through the use of Biometric Fingerprint, Facial or Palm recognition, desktop clocking, we allow organisations to positively identify individuals and easy employee management. Utilisations of biometric technologies assists with positive recognition of employees, preventing them from buddy clocking – common occurrence with manual registers.
